Frequently Asked Questions

What is GeM?
GeM is a centralized platform by the Indian Government for efficient, transparent, and paperless procurement of goods and services by government organizations.
Who can register as a Seller?
Manufacturers, traders, service providers, and professionals registered in India (Proprietorship, Partnership, LLP, Company) can register.
Is it mandatory for PSUs?
Yes, it is mandatory for central/state government departments and PSUs to procure goods/services through GeM for specified thresholds.
What documents are needed?
You will typically need PAN card, Aadhaar of owner, GST certificate, bank account details, and business registration proof.
