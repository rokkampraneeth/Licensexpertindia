Frequently Asked Questions

What is an LLP?
An LLP combines the benefits of a partnership (flexibility) and a company (limited liability). It is a separate legal entity where partners are not personally liable for the firm's debts.
What are the minimum requirements?
You need at least two designated partners, with at least one resident in India. There is no mandatory minimum capital requirement to start an LLP.
Is an audit mandatory?
Audit is mandatory only if annual turnover exceeds ₹40 lakhs or total capital contribution exceeds ₹25 lakhs. Otherwise, it is exempt.
Can an LLP convert to a Pvt Ltd?
Yes, an LLP can be converted into a Private Limited Company as per the Companies Act, 2013, subject to ROC filings and approvals.
